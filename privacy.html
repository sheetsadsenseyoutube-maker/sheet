Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Data Collection

We only collect the YouTube video URLs you provide and the activation keys associated with your account. We do not store any personal passwords or Google account details.

2. Processing Time (Important)

Once an activation key is successfully applied, our system registers your video for promotion. Please note that the entire process takes between 1 to 30 days to fully reflect on your YouTube channel due to organic rendering and algorithm updates.

3. Payment & Refunds

Keys are generated via direct contact (WhatsApp). Once a key is used, it binds to our system and cannot be reused or refunded. The promotional fee is currently discounted from ₹150 to ₹50.

4. Security

All interactions on this platform are encrypted. Your video analytics are fetched purely for display purposes during the session.
